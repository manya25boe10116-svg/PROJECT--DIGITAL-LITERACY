# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do’s
- **Think before posting**  
  Make sure your content is respectful, appropriate, and aligns with your values.

- **Protect your privacy**  
  Use privacy settings and avoid sharing sensitive information like phone numbers, addresses, or passwords.

- **Be respectful**  
  Communicate politely and respectfully, even when you disagree with others.

- **Verify information**  
  Check the authenticity of news or posts before sharing to prevent spreading misinformation.

- **Maintain a professional image**  
  Remember that your social media profile can be viewed by recruiters and future employers.

---

## ❌ Don’ts
- **Don’t overshare personal information**  
  Avoid posting private details that could compromise your safety or security.

- **Don’t engage in cyberbullying**  
  Never post or support harmful, offensive, or abusive content.

- **Don’t spread rumors or fake news**  
  Sharing unverified information can mislead others and damage reputations.

- **Don’t post impulsively**  
  Avoid posting when you are angry or emotional, as it may lead to regret later.

- **Don’t ignore your digital footprint**  
  Understand that everything you post online can remain permanently accessible.

---

## 📌 Conclusion
Using social media responsibly helps you build a positive online presence, stay safe, and maintain a good reputation both personally and professionally.
