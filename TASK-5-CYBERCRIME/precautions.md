# 🔐 Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist (India)

## ✅ General Safety Tips
- **Verify unknown contacts** before sharing any personal or financial information.  
- **Do not click on suspicious links** received via SMS, email, or social media.  
- **Use strong passwords** and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).  
- **Avoid public Wi-Fi** for sensitive transactions like banking or payments.  
- **Keep your apps and devices updated** to prevent security vulnerabilities.  
- **Limit personal information sharing** on social media platforms.  

## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- **Never share your UPI PIN or OTP** with anyone under any circumstances.  
- **Remember: entering UPI PIN means you are paying money**, not receiving it.  
- **Carefully check collect requests** before approving them.  
- **Use official apps only** (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) and avoid unknown payment links.  

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Helpline Number:** 1930  

> Report fraud immediately to increase the chances of recovering lost money.
